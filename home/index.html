Coleman Edwards

A Utah local, Coleman grew up in a musical family, with a brother on the sax, sister on guitar, and a dad on Oboe. He started playing piano when he was young, though he couldn't tell you the year. His first band was in 6th grade, where he played the guitar and sang on the band's school-talent-show single "You are my Beverage of Choice". Despite it's success (it won the principle's favor), the band broke up and he moved on with jazz piano lessons.

His next band began just before Red Yeti was formed, an electronic duo with a roommate at BYU. All was going well until the duo realized they hadn't written any music together. Once Red Yeti came along, the project was put on hold.

Coleman is the cousin of Kimball Barker. They often saw each other at yearly family reunions, and have fond memory of playing Tony Hawk's Pro Skater together in Coleman's basement. They started the band upon Coleman's conversation with Kimball's dad at a wedding reception for a mutual cousin…twice removed…it was all downhill from there.

Nick Blosil

If Kimball hadn't asked Nic to play drums for Red Yeti, he'd still be singing in a boy band called One Dimension. Classically trained on the piano, his high school job was playing for tips at a fine dining restaurant called Chef's Table. Nic was born in Switzerland and loves the smell of cows. It is a fact that people have been known to find answers to their deepest questions while watching Nics drumsticks pound the drums.

Jared Scott

Jared’s history is unknown. He is mysteries but feels like your best friend from the moment you begin speaking with him. He has been said to hail from the land where megladons still exist and Lygers run wild. He eats pure rock and roll for breakfast and snores in the key of C. Jared plays the bass.

Kimball Barker

When the zombie apocalypse happens, and you are stuck on the roof of your local grocery store with an axe as your last weapon, Kimball Barker is the person you want to be by your side. From his mouth, notes are sung that turn the most hardened criminal into a kind, loving, family man. He knows where both Waldo and Carmin Sandiego reside. Kimball is the lead singer of Red Yeti.

Isaac Lomeli

Isaac is…he just is.

The Band

There was a day: eyes opened, the sun rose, and somewhere in a small town located in the Western hemisphere a guitar note struck through the air turning the atmosphere around it into what can only be described by the word: euphoria. Red Yeti was born. Beginning with Kimball, Coleman, and Isaac, the band played for quite some time feeling something needed to be found. Enter Nic and Jared, stage right. The gap was filled and it was time for a blaze of musical experimentation to occur. Escaping to cabin in the middle of nowhere and sleeping a Yert while humming tunes like "What Yerts The Most" and the band found its sound. Red Yeti is here for you to hear Rock and Roll.
